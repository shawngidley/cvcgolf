League Rules

CVC Fantasy Golf 2026 - Official Rules

1. League Format

  • 14 players compete across 27 PGA Tour tournaments
  • Season: The Sentry (Jan) through FedEx St. Jude Championship (Aug)
  • Winner determined by total accumulated PGA Tour earnings from picked golfers
  • Dollar-for-dollar scoring based on official PGA Tour prize money

2. Weekly Lineups

  • Select 5 golfers each week
  • Total salary must not exceed $100 salary cap
  • Picks lock at the first tee time Thursday morning
  • No picks = $0 earnings for that week
  • No limit on how many times a golfer can be used per season

3. Scoring

  • Your weekly score = sum of official PGA Tour earnings from your 5 golfers
  • Missed cut = $0 earnings for that golfer
  • WD mid-tournament = keeps earned money up to that point
  • All earnings are real PGA Tour purse distributions

4. Season Prizes

1st Place
$500
2nd Place
$250
3rd Place
$100
Most Weekly Wins
$50

Entry fee: $75 per player • Total pot: $1,050

5. Commissioner

  • Commissioner enters tournament results each week via admin panel
  • Results are final once posted; standings auto-calculate
  • Commissioner has final say on disputes
  • Salary adjustments may occur mid-season based on OWGR changes
